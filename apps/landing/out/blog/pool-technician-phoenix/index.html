Frequently Asked Questions

How much does pool service cost?+
Weekly pool cleaning and chemical treatment typically runs $100–$200 per month. Equipment repairs vary widely: pump replacement $300–$800, filter replacement $150–$450, heater repair $200–$600. Pool resurfacing costs $3,500–$8,000 depending on size and material.
How often should my pool be serviced?+
Most pools in warm climates should be professionally serviced weekly during swim season. Pools used heavily or in high-algae-risk environments may need more frequent attention. Monthly visits for light-use or covered pools are sometimes sufficient in mild climates.
What causes green pool water?+
Green water is almost always algae, caused by low chlorine levels or pH imbalance. This can happen quickly, especially in hot weather. A professional pool service can typically clear a green pool in 3–7 days with shock treatment and proper chemical balancing.
How do I find a reliable pool service company?+
Look for companies with consistent Google reviews, clear pricing, and service contracts that specify what's included. Ask specifically whether they test and document water chemistry at each visit and whether the same technician will service your pool each time.
When should I resurface my pool?+
Most pool surfaces last 10–15 years before requiring resurfacing. Signs you need resurfacing include rough texture that scrapes skin, visible staining that won't brush away, structural cracks, or peeling and flaking plaster.
