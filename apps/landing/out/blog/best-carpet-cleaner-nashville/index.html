Frequently Asked Questions

How much does professional carpet cleaning cost?+
Carpet cleaning typically costs $25–$75 per room, with whole-home packages often running $150–$400. Pet treatment, stain treatment, and carpet protection are usually additional charges. Be cautious of very low prices that turn into bait-and-switch billing — always get a written quote.
How often should carpets be professionally cleaned?+
Most carpet manufacturers recommend professional cleaning every 12–18 months. Households with pets, children, or allergy sufferers benefit from cleaning every 6–12 months. High-traffic areas may need attention more frequently.
Will carpet cleaning remove pet odors?+
Professional hot water extraction combined with enzyme-based pet treatment can significantly reduce or eliminate pet odors. For severe urine contamination that has soaked into the padding, the padding may need to be replaced for complete odor elimination.
How long does carpet take to dry after cleaning?+
Professional truck-mounted steam cleaning typically produces dry carpets in 4–8 hours under normal conditions. Good ventilation and air circulation speeds drying. Portable machines tend to leave carpets wetter, extending dry time to 12–24 hours.
Is hot water extraction (steam cleaning) or dry cleaning better?+
For most residential carpets, hot water extraction provides a deeper, more thorough clean. Dry cleaning methods use less moisture but may not penetrate as deeply into carpet fibers. Most reputable carpet manufacturers recommend hot water extraction as the preferred cleaning method.
