Frequently Asked Questions

How much does pressure washing cost?+
Driveway cleaning typically runs $100–$250. House washing ranges from $200–$500 for an average home. Deck cleaning runs $100–$300. Complete property packages (house, driveway, walkways, deck) often cost $400–$700. Quotes should be provided after seeing the property.
What's the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?+
Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to blast away dirt and grime. Soft washing uses lower pressure combined with cleaning solutions to safely clean delicate surfaces like vinyl siding, stucco, and roofing without damaging them. A professional company uses the right method for each surface.
How often should I get pressure washing service?+
Most homes benefit from exterior cleaning once or twice per year. Humid climates with rapid mold growth may need quarterly cleaning. Driveways and high-traffic areas may need more frequent attention. Proactive cleaning prevents buildup that requires more aggressive treatment over time.
Can pressure washing damage my home?+
In inexperienced hands, yes — too much pressure on wood, vinyl siding, or older brick can cause real damage. Always hire a company with demonstrated experience on your specific surface type. Soft washing for siding and roofs is much safer than high-pressure methods for delicate surfaces.
Should I pressure wash my roof?+
Roofs should be soft-washed, not pressure-washed. High pressure can strip granules from asphalt shingles, voiding warranties and reducing roof life. Soft washing with appropriate algaecide removes algae and staining safely and keeps it clean longer than pressure alone.
