Frequently Asked Questions

How much does professional window cleaning cost?+
Residential window cleaning typically runs $150–$400 depending on the number of windows, stories, and whether interior cleaning is included. Most companies price per pane or per window. Hard water stain removal is often an additional charge.
How often should windows be professionally cleaned?+
Most homeowners benefit from professional cleaning 2–4 times per year. Coastal properties near salt air may need monthly cleaning. Properties with sprinkler systems that hit windows benefit from more frequent cleaning to prevent hard water buildup.
Can professional window cleaning remove hard water stains?+
Yes — professional techniques using specialized acidic solutions, polishing compounds, and purified water systems can remove most hard water mineral deposits. Severe long-term buildup may require multiple treatments or glass restoration techniques.
Do professional window cleaners clean screens too?+
Most professional window cleaning services include screen cleaning or offer it as an add-on. Well-maintained screens allow better airflow and extend their lifespan — ask your provider about screen and track cleaning as part of any window cleaning package.
Is it safe to have windows cleaned in winter?+
Professional window cleaners work year-round in most climates using water-fed pole systems and solutions appropriate for the temperature. In freezing conditions, exterior cleaning may not be advisable, but interior-only cleaning can always proceed.
