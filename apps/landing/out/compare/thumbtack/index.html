What is Thumbtack?

Thumbtack is a project-based lead marketplace where contractors pay to quote on homeowner jobs. Unlike Angi's membership model, Thumbtack charges per quote sent — meaning you pay whether or not you win the job.

What they say: Thumbtack lets contractors set their own availability and pricing, only paying for leads that match their services — with a transparent pay-per-quote model and free profile listing.

Frequently asked questions

Does Thumbtack charge if I don't get the job?

Yes. Thumbtack charges per quote sent, not per job won. You pay for every quote regardless of whether the homeowner hires you.

How many contractors compete for the same Thumbtack job?

Thumbtack typically sends each homeowner request to 3–5 contractors. In competitive markets, more contractors may be quoting the same job.

What happens to my Thumbtack reviews if I switch?

They stay on Thumbtack's platform. You can't take them with you. This is one of the core risks of building a reputation on someone else's platform.
