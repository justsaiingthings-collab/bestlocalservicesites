What is ServiceTitan?

ServiceTitan is the enterprise-grade field service management platform used by large HVAC, plumbing, and electrical companies. It manages scheduling, dispatch, marketing, and financials at scale. It is not designed for independent contractors or small shops — and its pricing reflects that.

What they say: ServiceTitan provides the trades industry with an end-to-end platform for managing every aspect of a field service business, from marketing and call booking to dispatch, invoicing, and reporting.

Frequently asked questions

Is ServiceTitan appropriate for a solo contractor or small shop?

No. ServiceTitan is designed for companies with multiple technicians and complex scheduling needs. The pricing ($400+/month per tech) and implementation complexity make it inappropriate for most contractors under $500K revenue.

Does ServiceTitan help with Google Local Pack rankings?

ServiceTitan includes marketing features, but its website tools are not optimized for Local Pack rankings in the way that BLSS's schema markup, city pages, and mobile performance optimization are.
