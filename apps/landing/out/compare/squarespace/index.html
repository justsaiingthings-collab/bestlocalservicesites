Frequently asked questions

Does Squarespace support contractor-specific schema markup?

Squarespace provides basic meta tags but does not automatically generate LocalBusiness, Service, or FAQPage schema — the three types that most directly impact Local Pack rankings for contractors.

Can I add a sticky mobile call bar to Squarespace?

Yes, but it requires custom CSS/JavaScript injection. Most contractors either can't do this themselves or pay a developer to add it — an additional cost that brings the total closer to BLSS pricing.

Why does page speed matter for contractor websites?

Google uses mobile page speed as a ranking factor, and homeowners searching during an emergency (burst pipe, AC out) will leave a slow site immediately. BLSS targets 90+ Lighthouse scores; Squarespace typically scores 60–80.
