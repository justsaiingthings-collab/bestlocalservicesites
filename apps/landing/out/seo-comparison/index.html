Schema.org: LocalBusiness vs. Article

Traditional SEO focuses on 'Article' schema to rank blogs. We use 'LocalBusiness' + 'ServiceArea' JSON-LD. This code tells Google exactly which zip codes you service, making you the undisputed local authority for AI search queries.

Review Velocity vs. Review Count

A business with 500 reviews from 3 years ago will lose to a business with 50 reviews and 2 new ones this week. We focus on 'Velocity'—the constant stream of new social proof that signals 'Freshness' to the algorithm.
